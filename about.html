About us

Toys "AR" Us Is a Belgian company that started in 2022 as part of a school assignment. The webshop quickly grew into one of the worlds biggest exporters of action figures. Whats the secret to our succes? Augmented reality. The fact that the user can watch the merchandise in his/her living room before buying it was a mayor selling point.
